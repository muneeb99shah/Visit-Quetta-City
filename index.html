Sajji is a leg of lamb. It is really tasty and nutritious food by Balochistani people. Sajji is marinated with local herbs and masalas and then it is cooked like barbecue in an open fire. Sajji is a good food which shows the high consumption of fat by people living in cold areas like Quetta, Baluchistan.
